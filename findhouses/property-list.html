Best Places to Live in Bangalore

The ever-expanding chic city of Bangalore offers residential flats in different ranges. You can find individual flats in Bangalore in a range of less than Rs 5,000 per sqft to those above Rs 7,500 per sqft. You can find flats in posh localities and in more modest areas too.


Bangalore has seen a huge expansion in the real estate market for flats in the last 2-3 decades. This trend had seen stagnation for the two-three years of the pandemic. But the market has picked up again and the growth trends visible in the previous decades are expected to continue in the future.


If you are planning to buy a flat in Bangalore, here are some areas to choose from:

1 - Kanakapura Road Vakil Garden City

If you are looking for a flat in Bangalore that comes with the benefit of both a serene environment and proximity to IT hubs, then Kanakapura Road is the best. The area is home to a number of high-end residential projects equipped with all the modern amenities. This includes Godrej Eternity, Mantri Tranquil, Prestige Falcon City, and Brigade Meadows, to name a few. Excellent metro connectivity and availability of Cauvery water further make this locality more favorable. Kanakapura Road is also well-connected to Banashankari, Outer Ring Road, Bannerghatta Road, Electronic City, and Jayanagar. The demand for flats in Bangalore on Kanakapura Road has been fueled by the presence of numerous well-established schools, top hospitals, and well-known shopping malls nearby.
